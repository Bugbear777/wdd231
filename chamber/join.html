Non-Profit Membership Details

Our Non-Profit Membership is tailored for organizations dedicated to making a positive impact in our community. This membership level provides access to networking events, educational resources, and opportunities to collaborate with local businesses, all at no cost.

  • Access to networking events
  • Educational resources and workshops
  • Opportunities for collaboration with local businesses
  • Inclusion in our online directory
×

Bronze Membership Details

The Bronze Membership is perfect for small businesses looking to increase their visibility within the local business community. This membership level includes a basic listing in our directory, invitations to select networking events, and access to member-only resources.

  • Basic listing in our online directory
  • Invitations to select networking events
  • Access to member-only resources and newsletters
  • Discounts on workshops and seminars
×

Silver Membership Details

The Silver Membership is designed for growing businesses that want to enhance their presence in the community. This membership level offers an enhanced directory listing, priority invitations to events, and access to exclusive webinars and training sessions.

  • Enhanced listing in our online directory
  • Priority invitations to networking events
  • Access to exclusive webinars and training sessions
  • Opportunities for sponsorship and advertising
×

Gold Membership Details

The Gold Membership is our premier offering, ideal for established businesses seeking maximum exposure and engagement. This membership level includes premium placement in our directory, VIP access to events, and personalized support from our team.

  • Premium placement in our online directory
  • VIP access to all networking events
  • Personalized support and consultation
  • Opportunities for featured articles and interviews
